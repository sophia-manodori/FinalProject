_____ 2 pts: Dataset is selected and fully described in `data_description.txt`
_____ 2 pts: Journal includes at least one entry per phase and describes the design decisions made and reasons for making them
_____ 2 pts: The program reads the data in and stores them in the form of a graph
_____ 2 pts: Graph is used to compute new information, including something significant about node connectivity.
_____ 2 pts: The program's output communicates the results in a clear and unambiguous manner
_____ 2 pts: The program is designed in a general way so that it would work for any dataset that follows the same format and could solve any problem of the category that your program is solving (e.g., nodes are not hardcoded)
